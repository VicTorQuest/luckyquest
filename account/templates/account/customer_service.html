If you have requested a withdrawal but have not yet received the funds, there could be several reasons for this.

Withdrawal Pending, Withdrawal Processing, Withdrawal in Progress: These messages indicate that the bank is still processing the transaction. The withdrawal may be completed as quickly as within 1 minute, and the funds should be credited to your account within a maximum of 72 hours. Please be patient.

Withdrawal Completed: If you requested a withdrawal on the same day and it has been submitted to the bank, with the bank confirming completion, there may be a delay in the funds reaching your account.

Withdrawal Rejected: There could be various reasons for this. Please check the rejection reason. A withdrawal may be rejected due to a bank error, exceeding the time limit, or surpassing the bank's maximum transaction limit. You don’t need to worry; please apply for the withdrawal again.

Important Notice: If you requested a withdrawal on the same day and received a completion notice, but the funds have not yet reached your bank account, there may be a delay. If more than 72 hours have passed and you still haven’t received the funds, there’s no need to worry. You can contact customer service and provide relevant bank information, PDF files, video proof, etc., to resolve the issue
